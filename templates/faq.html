Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about our veterinary services at Animal Clinic:

What vaccinations does my pet need?

Vaccinations vary depending on the type of pet and their age. Please contact our office for a consultation to determine the best vaccine plan for your pet.

Can I bring my pet in for a checkup?

Yes! We offer routine checkups and examinations for all of our patients. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

What services does your clinic offer?

We offer a wide range of services including general checkup, vaccinations, dental care, and more. Please contact us for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept both payment through Khalti. For booking an appointment, you have to do an advance payment of Rs 500. This money won't be returned even after cancellation.
